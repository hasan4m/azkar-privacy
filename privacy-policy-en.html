Privacy Policy

Azkar Al-Noom (Sleep Adhkar) app  •  Last updated: July 12, 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what information the “Azkar Al-Noom” app (the “App”) handles and how it is used. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information. We do not operate any servers that store your data.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves certain preferences locally on your device only (using local storage). This data is never transmitted to us or any third party. It includes:

  • Adhkar and tasbeeh added to your favorites.
  • Personal adhkar you add in the “My Adhkar” section.
  • Your daily nightly wird (routine) progress.
  • Appearance preferences: app color, and font type, size, and color.
  • The reciter selected in listening mode.

Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all of this data.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads through Google AdMob. Google and its partners may collect data such as your Advertising ID, IP address, and general device information in order to serve, measure, and protect against fraud in advertising. The App itself does not access or store this data.

You can learn how Google handles data at:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in advertising

4. User Consent (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, the App presents a consent form (Google UMP) before showing personalized ads, allowing you to accept or reject personalized advertising. Your choice is respected and stored on your device.

5. Audio Recitation Playback

When you play the recitation of Surah Al-Mulk in “Listening Mode,” the audio is streamed from public Quran recitation servers. This requires an internet connection and may expose your device’s IP address to those servers, as is inherent to any network connection. The App does not send any additional personal data.

6. Permissions

The App uses only the internet access permission, for showing ads and streaming audio recitation. The App does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, files, or any sensitive data.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience, does not target children under the age of 13, and does not knowingly collect any data from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated “Last updated” date above.
